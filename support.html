Common questions

  • Are the rates real-time? Rates are indicative and refreshed periodically. Always verify with the provider before making a decision.
  • Is YieldScope a broker or financial advisor? No. YieldScope is an information-comparison tool only. See our privacy policy and the in-app disclaimer for details.
  • How do I delete my data? The app does not collect any personal data. Your watchlist is stored only on your device — uninstalling the app removes it.
